WEATHER

Winter

1.Gulmarg , Kashmir

Location: Baramulla district of Jammu and Kashmir, India, in the Western Himalayas
Speciality:Known as Meadow of Flowers and has the world's highest golf course , Cable car ride
Temperature: -7.8 to 0.6 degree celsius
Visit between : December to February

2. Shimla-Kufri, Himachal Pradesh

Location: 20 kilometers from Shimla on National Highway No. 22
Speciality: surrounded by rolling hills and snow-covered A popular destination for snowfall, with
adventure activities and panoramic views of the Himalayas forests,
Temperature: 0 to 13 celsius
Visit between :November to March

3. Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Location: Town in the Kullu district of Himachal Pradesh, India, located in the northern end of the Kullu Valley
Speciality: Snow-covered mountains and trees, and activities like biking trip to Rohtang Pass,
paragliding in Solang Valley or hiking up to Jogini Waterfalls
Temperature: -5 to 6 celcius
Visit between:October–February

4. Auli, Uttarakhand

Location: Chamoli district of Uttarakhand, India, in the Himalayan mountains
Speciality: numerous ski resorts dotting the region are bustling with holiday makers, and adventure
junkies are on their way to tame the snow-laden slopes.
Temperature: -8 to 10 celsius
Visit between: November to February

5. Binsar, Uttarakhand

Location: in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand, India, about 30 kilometers north of Almora
Speciality: small hill station with a wildlife sanctuary, temples, meadows, and views of the Himalayan peaks
Temperature: 2 to 17 celsius
Visit between: December to February

6.Dalhousie, Himachal Pradesh

Location: a hill station in the Chamba district of Himachal Pradesh, India, located in the Himalayan foothills
Speciality: A hike through the Deodar forest,  National Himalayan Winter Trekking Expedition.
Temperature: 7.7 to 16 celsius
Visit between: October to February

7.Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh

Location: 448 km northwest of Itanagar, the state capital
Speciality: Urgelling Monastery – the birthplace of the 6th Dalai Lama, glorious views of the
snow-covered Himalayas, valleys wrapped by colourful prayer flags and ancient monasteries, Torgya and Losar Festivals
Temperature: 2 to 7 celsius
Visit between: November to January

8. Gangtok, Sikkim

Location: Capital of the state Sikkim
Speciality: trip to Nathula Pass and Tsomgo Lake, Rich flora and fauna, tranquil high-altitude lakes,
colourful monasteries and views of the surreal Himalayas
Temperature: 7.8 to 14.6 celsius
Visit between: October to february

9. Shillong, Meghalaya

Location: east-central part of the state of Meghalaya in northeastern India
Speciality: referred to as ‘The Scotland of the East’, Lady Hydari Park, Don Bosco Centre for Indigenous Cultures,
Ward’s Lake, and Elephant Falls, cleanest village in Asia, Mawylnnong.
Temperature: 9 to 17 celsius
Visit between: November to February

10. Ooty , Tamil Nadu

Location: the Nilgiris district of Tamil Nadu, India
Speciality: emnants of the colonial period like St. Stephen’s Church. In winters,
the town’s famous coffee plantations and dense flora are covered by mist, which gives it a dreamy and romantic ambience, A ride on the Nilgiri Mountain Toy Train
Temperature: 11.6 to 20 celsius
Visit between: October to february
